How to connect?

  • First of all you need to sen port forwarding!

  • Tap on one of this links (which one will work): http://192.168.0.1/ or http://192.168.1.1

  • Authorize > Settings > Port Forwarding > Add

  • Set a name, pick your device, Internal port: 1234, External port: 1234, Protocols: Everything

  • Download providing software and enter your own IP in according field. (IP'll be represented in program)

  • Start using your computer by remote control from devices you want ^_^
